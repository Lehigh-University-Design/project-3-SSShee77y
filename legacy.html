Aviation Security

Anti-Hijacking Protocols

Cooper's hijacking, and several copycat hijackers, prompted major upgrades to security measures for airports and commercial aviation. The U.S. government established the first anti-hijacking security protocols, preventing hijackers from getting on aircraft in the first place. The measures included a hijacker profile, metal detectors, X-ray machines, baggage inspection became mandatory, and passengers who paid cash for tickets on the day of departure were selected for additional scrutiny.

These protocols put in place in response to hijackings in the 1970s helped laid the foundation of modern aviation security standards.

Aerial Retrofits

Boeing 727s and similar aircraft were retrofitted with "Cooper vanes" to specifically prevent the ventral stairs of the aircraft from being lowered. It consists of a spring-loaded paddle connected to a plate that, as the aircraft takes off, the airflow pushes the paddle parallel to the fuselage and the plate is moved underneath the stairway, preventing the stairs from being lowered.

Pop Culture & Media

Skyborne Robin Hood

D.B. Cooper's daring heist captured the public's imagination and became a cultural phenomenon, inspiring numerous depictions and references in media, literature, music, and art, and each interpretation offering its own speculation and theories. The enduring allure of the D.B. Cooper case has cemented its place in American folklore, with the hijacker's name becoming synonymous with mystery and intrigue. Some even compare Cooper to a modern day robin hood, peculating that he may have used it to help others or support a cause.

Cooper Con

CooperCon is an annual gathering of enthusiasts, investigators, authors, and experts dedicated to unraveling the mysteries surrounding the D.B. Cooper case. Founded in 2011 by Eric Ulis, CooperCon provides a forum for individuals interested in the case to come together and collaborate on the case.

The event also features presentations, panel discussions, and workshops on various aspects of the case, including theories, evidence, and potential leads. CooperCon fosters a sense of community among those passionate about the D.B. Cooper mystery.
